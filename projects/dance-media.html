Visualize moves through wearable sensors (Hackation project).

Links

Demo Video (Vimeo)

Goal

This hackathon project visualizes subtle movements of the dancers, enabling dancing learners to have a better grasp of dancers' movement and enhancing spectators' experience.

Description

In a two-day hackathon, our team of two wired up homebrewed feet pressure sensors and movement sensors, and programmed Arduino microcontroller to display strength of movements with LED strips. This wearable device can also collect data for later use or other types of visualization.
