Goal

This game is designed around provide players the feeling of relief by experimenting the idea of "punching" and "feedback". As a winter class project which has no longer than 2 weeks' development time, this project also let us (developers) experiment with ideas of play, trying out character design, and learning ios programming and Cocos 2d.
