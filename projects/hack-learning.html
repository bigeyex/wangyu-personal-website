Tell children what works in the future look like (Hackathon project).

Goal

Students in schools seldom have a concept of what the myriad of careers out in the world look like.

Description

This project (in prototype stage) curated a list of videos of actual working scenes in the workplaces. Students can match their interest by searching, clicking on popular tags, or using sliders denoting characteristics of works.
