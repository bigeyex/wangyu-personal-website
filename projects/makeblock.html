Goal

The mission of this project is assisting Chinese grassroots NGOs in improving their digital and social media literacy, enhancing their capability for social innovation, and developing the concept and practice of technology aided social service sector by building cross-sector partnerships.

Description

Started from 2009, NGO2.0 project has evolved into 12 sub-projects, addressing NGOs' communication, technology, and resource needs. Example of sub-projects includes a crowdsourced map for cross-sector discovery of events and organizations, training workshops across various provinces in China, and a collection of technology tools for social change.

Team

This project is built upon a coalition of universities and foundations, and a consulting committee of more than 10 people. For more details, see "Team Members" page

My Role

I am the chief engineer of this project, responsible for all technology infrastructure. I designed (with cooperation from designers from Guangzhou Sun-Yet Sun University) and developed the web platform. I'm also an instructor in 2 workshops, and survey designer/data analyst in the NGO2.0 project.
