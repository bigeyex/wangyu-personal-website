Goal

develop an ios app to visualize and collect data expressed by audio frequency signals.

Description

Public Lab have a project that transform water conductivity readings to sound signals, allowing children to "listen to" conductivity of the water and have a acoustic feeling of data. This app further allow people to plug a wire in there cellphone to have a visualized understanding of sound frequencies, collect data in a certain period, and calculate the error margins.
