Goal

Providing intimate storytelling experience through wearable technology

Description

In this project for class "Designing Interaction", our team has gone through market and technology research, ethnographic studies, conceptual design, and prototype development. We choose Motorola's Moto Hint (an bone-conduction always-listening earbud) as the wearable technology and designed a companion app to fulfill the seamless urban storytelling experience.

Team
